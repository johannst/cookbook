# Pizza Dough

## Ingredients
For `one baking tray`:
- flour `250g`
- water (lukewarm) `125ml`
- salt `1 tsp`
- sugar `1 pinch`
- dry yeast `1/2 pkg (~3.5g)`

## Instructions
1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
1. Add the water and mix.
1. Knead the dough for about `5-10min`.
1. Let the dough rest for about `1h` at a warm place (`~30C`).
